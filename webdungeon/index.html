About the Game

WebDungeon is a strategic tower defense game with a twist - you're defending the dungeon! As the dungeon keeper, you must protect your core from waves of invading heroes using a variety of traps and defensive structures.

Gameplay

Each match begins with a procedurally generated dungeon layout, creating unique strategic challenges every time. You'll have a preparation phase to place your initial defenses before the first wave begins. Between waves, you'll receive additional resources to expand your defenses and adapt to increasingly difficult enemy formations.

Controls:
  • 1 - Select Spike Trap (high damage, limited uses)
  • 2 - Select Slow Trap (reduces hero movement speed)
  • 3 - Select Blocker (blocks pathways, must be destroyed)
  • Left Click - Place selected trap

Key Features

Procedural Generation

Every dungeon is uniquely generated with rooms, corridors, and multiple spawn points, ensuring no two games play the same.

A* Pathfinding

Heroes use intelligent pathfinding to navigate to your core, adapting their routes when blockers are placed or destroyed.

Resource Management

Traps are limited and refill between waves. Strategic placement and timing are essential for survival.

Trap Durability System

Most traps have limited uses before breaking, forcing you to constantly adapt your defensive strategy.

Dynamic Combat

Heroes will engage and destroy blockers in their path, creating evolving battlefield conditions.

Wave-Based Difficulty

Each wave spawns more heroes than the last, with exponential scaling that tests your strategic planning.

Technical Systems

  • Dungeon Generation: BSP-based room generation with corridor connections
  • Pathfinding: A* algorithm with blocker-aware routing
  • Grid System: Tile-based placement and movement using Unity's Tilemap
  • Event-Driven Architecture: Decoupled systems communicate via C# events
  • Resource Management: Wave-based refill system with per-trap-type limits
  • Combat System: Trigger-based trap activation with durability tracking
