About

I'm a Unity developer and technical lead specializing in complex 3D applications and game development. With a BSc in Games and Applications Development and years of professional experience, I've led teams through challenging technical projects from conception to delivery.

Most recently, I spent 5 years as team lead and lead programmer on a sophisticated 3D interior design application, managing both technical architecture and team coordination. This experience taught me how to balance innovation with reliability, and how to deliver complex software that users love.

I'm passionate about creating polished, performant applications and believe in rapid prototyping to validate ideas quickly. Whether you need a game prototype, a 3D tool, or technical leadership for your project, I bring both hands-on expertise and strategic thinking to the table.
